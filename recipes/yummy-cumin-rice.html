Yummy Cumin Rice Recipe

Description

This rice dish is a rich and aromatic accompaniment to any meal. It's so incredibly delicious that it may even overshadow the main dish. Even better, it seems fancy and intricate but is actually quite quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

Rice pot

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 Indian bay leaf
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 1 black cardamom
  • 1/2 inch cinnamon
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 to 2 strands of mace
  • 1 tsp salt

Tempering

  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp green chilli, chopped
  • 1 to 2 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped

Steps

Cooking Rice

  1. Soak rice for half an hour.
  2. Drain water. Rice rice until the water runs clear.
  3. Put the rice in your saucepan. Add water (2 cups) and all whole spices apart from those reserved for tempering. Add salt.
  4. Leave rice uncovered on a medium boil until water is almost all evaporated. Then cover the saucepan and turn off the stove.
  5. Rice will need to sit for at least 10 minutes to absorb all remaining moisture, soften and cool.

Making Tempering

  1. Heat ghee in a small pan. Add cumin.
  2. Let cumin seeds crackle very briefly. Don't let them burn.
  3. Add the green chillies. Stir quickly, then remove the pan from the heat.

Serving

  1. Add the tempering mix to the rice, gently stirring it through.
  2. Add coriander to the rice mix
  3. Serve cumin rice warm with your choice of main.
